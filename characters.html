Characters

Meet the Monkeys

AiAi

Nickname: The OG

Speed: High

Balance: High

Power: Medium

AiAi is the main protagonist since the first game and the most well-rounded character. Perfect for players who want a versatile monkey that can handle any situation.

MeeMee

Nickname: The Swift

Speed: Very High

Balance: Very High

Power: Low

MeeMee has highest speed and the second best balance of stats, she excels at navigating tight spaces and quick turns.

GonGon

Nickname: The Powerhouse

Speed: Medium

Balance: Medium

Power: Very High

GonGon brings alot of power to the team. With the highest power stat, he can break through obstacles and maintain momentum better than other characters.

Baby

Nickname: The Baby

Speed: High

Balance: Low

Power: High

Baby has good speed and power and rewards skillful players who can work around the slight handling difficulty. The smaller ball can really help on some balancing obstacles
