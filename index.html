The Ultimate Monkey Adventure

Welcome

Super Monkey Ball is a fast-paced arcade game where you guide monkeys inside transparent balls through challenging obstacle courses. The goal is to reach the end of each stage without falling off. This game requires skill and patience, especially on harder levels. Every stage gets more intense as you progress.

Game Modes

Mode Description
Story Mode Complete levels through 10 different worlds.
Golden Banana Take on 10 stages and collect all golden bananas in each stage.
Time Attack Beat preset stages as fast as possible.
